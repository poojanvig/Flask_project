Shree Mahalakshmi Constructions

A Legacy Of Years Driven By A Desire

Development is the cornerstone of Shree Mahalakshmi Constructions' success. Over the years, the company has fine-tuned its approach to key development components such as site selection, location, plot size, design, and value generation. Founded in 2013 as a Private Partnership Company, we have meticulously shaped our identity, mission, and purpose.
At Shree Mahalakshmi Constructions, we prioritize adopting the highest business traits, ensuring that every engagement nurtures long-term relationships with landowners, customers, investors, and other business partners.

Vision & Mission

Our mission and vision at Shree Mahalakshmi Constructions stand firm -

Our mission is to create meaningful and extraordinary homes through a professional, informative, transparent, and dedicated team. We aim to exceed our clients' expectations and serve them better than the rest.

Our vision is to establish ourselves as the most trusted and transparent leader in the real estate industry. We aim to build lasting relationships with our clients by consistently delivering unparalleled service, fostering a culture of honesty, and ensuring complete transparency in all our transactions.

Leadership

We believe that the journey towards excellence begins with people who drive our strategies through collectively evolved processes, ensuring customer delight. We recruit the best and meaningfully engage with them resulting in a passionate, customer-centric, and loyal team. We consciously provide planned learning and developmental opportunities to the vast pool of talent that constitute our workforce. We build, sustain, and persistently reinforce a company culture that reflects a high-performance atmosphere rooted in trust and reliability, professional ethics, and innovation.

Key Management Personnel

Hiren Sunil Wadhare

Founder & Managing Partner

He holds a Master’s and Bachelor’s degree in Management Studies from the University of Mumbai. Coming from a Family Business Background, Mr. Hiren Sunil Wadhare is a third-generation property developer who had been with his grandfather, father and uncle on their family construction sites from his childhood days which helped him gain an experience of onsite work and thereby being a good listener and an excellent observer which helped him start Shree Mahalakshmi Constructions at the age of 23. He has 2 years of experience for working in Abu Dhabi Commercial Bank at Dubai as a Relationship Officer that has helped him acquire amazing sales technique and handle difficult situation or queries in dealing with Investors, Land Owners and Customers. He has 10+ years of experience in various aspects of Real Estate Business.

Mrs. Leena Sunil Wadhare

Founder & Partner

She holds a bachelor’s degree in Commerce from the University of Mumbai. She has been associated with the company since its incorporation. She has over 25 years of experience in different aspects of her entrepreneurial journey and 10 years of experience in various aspects from real estate business.

Mr. Sunil Meghraj Wadhare

Co- Founder & Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Sunil Meghraj Wadhare is the Chief Executive Officer at Shree Mahalakshmi Constructions where he leads all the site management and operations for Mumbai. He has been associated with the company since its incorporation. He has overall 40 years of experience in the different aspects of business. Mr. Sunil Started his career by starting a Steel Manufacturing unit in the outskirts of Mumbai which is an essential product for the construction of buildings. He has vast experience of more than 35+ years in the steel industry and was also managing 3 steel manufacturing units spanning over an acre each simultaneously.
