Shree Sumitra Apartments, located in Liberty Garden, Malad West, Mumbai, is a completed residential project with 14 flats spread across a low-rise structure. The project, covering a net plot area of 225 sq. meters, was constructed over two years from December 2016 to December 2018.

The project offers a grand entrance lobby, high-speed elevator, gymnasium, independent car parking, and a range of premium internal fittings like polished glazed vitrified tiles and anodized French windows.
